📖 How to mark your attendance

1
Download the mobile app Install the QR Attendance app on your Android or iOS device.
2
Sign in with your student credentials Use the same email and password you log into this portal with.
3
Scan the QR code shown by your teacher Open the scanner in the app and point your camera at the QR code on the board or screen.
4
Confirm — you're marked present! The system will instantly record your attendance. You can view it below.
⏱️

QR codes expire

Each QR code is valid for 20 minutes. Make sure to scan it promptly when your teacher displays it.

🔒

One scan per session

You can only be marked present once per session. Duplicate scans are safely ignored.
